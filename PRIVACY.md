# Privacy Policy for Text Scanner

**Effective Date:** June 8, 2026

## Data Collection and Use
Text Scanner uses your device's camera to scan business cards and documents. The images are sent to secure servers (Alibaba Cloud) for text extraction. No images are stored after processing.

## Permissions Used
- **Camera**: Required to scan documents.
- **Internet**: Required to send images to the server.
- **Storage**: Required to save exported CSV files.

## Data Extracted
The app extracts the following information from scanned images:
- Name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Website URL
- Notes

## Third-Party Services
We use Alibaba Cloud API for text extraction. Your data is handled according to their privacy policy.

## Data Storage
All extracted data is stored only on your device. You control what gets saved and can delete it anytime.
